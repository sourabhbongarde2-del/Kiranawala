Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated:
⚠️ Ye generic template hai. Payment gateway (Razorpay/Cashfree) use karne se pehle apni policy ko unke requirements ke hisaab se update karo.

Cancellation

  • Order place hone ke turant baad (packing shuru hone se pehle) cancel kiya ja sakta hai — store ko WhatsApp/phone par batao
  • Ek baar order "Out for Delivery" status mein aa jaye, cancellation possible nahi hoga

Refund — COD Orders

Cash on Delivery orders mein, agar cancel ho jaye delivery se pehle, koi refund process nahi karna padta kyunki payment abhi hui hi nahi thi.

Refund — UPI Orders

  • Agar aapne UPI se pay kar diya aur order cancel karna hai, store aapko usi UPI ID par 3-5 business days mein refund karega
  • Damaged/wrong item milne par, product wapas karke ya photo bhej ke store se refund/replacement maango

Return Policy

Perishable items (dairy, vegetables, fruits) generally return nahi hote unless damaged/expired mile. Non-perishable items delivery ke 24 ghante ke andar return ho sakte hain agar seal/packaging kharab na hui ho.

Contact

Refund/cancellation ke liye seedha store se WhatsApp/phone par contact karo (details store page par milenge).
